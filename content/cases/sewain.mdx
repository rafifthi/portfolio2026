## Overview

Sewain is a property rental management system I'm driving as an R&D initiative. The idea is to give landlords and property managers one place to handle listings, tenants, and the day-to-day of running a rental portfolio — without bolting together a spreadsheet, a messaging app, and a calendar.

It's an early concept, but there's already a **live preview** you can poke at: [sewain-orchre.vercel.app](https://sewain-orchre.vercel.app).

## What I own

As the PM on the R&D side, I own the whole arc from discovery to MVP:

- Ran product discovery — who actually manages rentals, and where the pain really is
- Took the initiative from discovery to a working MVP
- Defined the core entities (properties, units, tenants, leases) and their relationships
- Orchestrated the build with an **agentic AI setup** — using Hermes Agent to accelerate the pipeline from spec to implementation

---

## Why now

Rental management is one of those problems that looks trivial until you're in it. Late rent, overlapping bookings, lost documents, tenants you can't reach — the operational noise adds up fast. The bet with Sewain is that a focused MVP, designed around the real workflow instead of a generic CRM, can take most of that noise off a landlord's plate.

---

## Where it's headed

The live preview is the first slice: enough to see the model and the core flows. From here it's about tightening the rental lifecycle — onboarding a property, matching a tenant, managing a lease — and letting the agentic pipeline carry more of the build so iteration stays cheap.
