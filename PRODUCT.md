# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The portfolio owner uses the private admin panel to maintain published portfolio content. The primary job is to add, remove, and organize content quickly without unnecessary metadata entry.

## Product Purpose

Provide a focused CMS inside the portfolio application. Success means routine content updates are fast, understandable, and reflected accurately on the public site, with the Gallery optimized as a visual image-management workflow.

## Brand Personality

Simple, familiar, and efficient. The admin should feel calm and dependable, with controls that disappear into the task.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboard patterns, excessive cards, hidden actions, unfamiliar controls, and form-heavy workflows for content that does not require metadata.

## Design Principles

1. Match the editor to the content type instead of forcing every module into one form pattern.
2. Prefer direct manipulation for inherently visual tasks such as uploading and ordering photos.
3. Keep primary actions obvious and routine maintenance fast.
4. Preserve familiar controls and consistent feedback across the admin panel.
5. Remove fields and decisions that do not affect the public result.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain clear focus states, usable keyboard controls, descriptive labels, sufficient contrast, and reduced-motion-safe feedback. Drag interactions must have an accessible non-drag alternative.
